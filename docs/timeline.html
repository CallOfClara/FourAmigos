New York

Joseph, Nick, Rosalita, and Abdul board R.M.S Olympic for England.

Friday, 23. January 1925

Onboard R.M.S Olympic

Nick starts learning alchemy. Rosie and Joseph start researching the tomes found from Peru and New York. Abdul drinks his worries away.

Saturday, 24. January 1925

Onboard R.M.S Olympic

The investigators play Bingo. Although Nick wins some money, the party fails to win the main prize (a camera).

Sunday, 25. January 1925

Onboard R.M.S Olympic

Joseph is surprised to meet Liam on the ship. Rosie learns about first aid. Nick does aerobics.

Tuesday, 27. January 1925

Onboard R.M.S Olympic

Rosie borrows a book from Nick and has a breakthrough! Joseph and Nick meet up with Liam - they end up joining Tenebrarum Nexus.

Wednesday, 28. January 1925

Onboard R.M.S Olympic

Nick joins a relaxing evening of poetry. Rosie delves into astronomy. Joseph goes boxing.

Thursday, 29. January 1925

Onboard R.M.S Olympic

Joseph wins a shooting competition and wins a nice prize. R.M.S Olypic reaches Liverpool after 7 days of travel.

Saturday, 31. January 1925

England - London

Investigators check into a Hotel in london and settle in. Nick obtains a library card. Rosie and Joseph visit the Scoop and meet Mickey Mahoney. Nick and Rosie visit the Penhew Foundation.

Sunday, 1. February 1925

England - London

Rosie visits Penhew Foundation and meets Edward Gavigan. Nick and Joseph visit Scotland Yard and meet inspector Barrington. Afterwards Nick and Joseph try and find out more about Miles Shipley and end up killing a beast! Nick and Rosie visit Penhew foundation again. Rosie visits the library.

Monday, 2. February 1925

England - London

Abdul and Rosie bond over fixing a car. The trio revisits the house of Shipley and discover a hidden room in the basement. Afterwards, the investigators visit the Blue Pyramid Club. Joseph meets distressed Yalesha and ends up dragging Nick into a fight. Yalesha gets dragged into Blue Pyramid Club.

Tuesday, 3. February 1925

England - London

Joseph is concerned about Yalesha and decides to stake out the club with Abdul. Nick and Rosie get followed by some mean looking thugs. Nick tries to confront them but they get away. Joseph notices a car belonging to Ferrys & Sons and decides to follow it to Penhew foundation and then Essex. In the meantime, Rosie visits Penhew Foundation to write up a proposal for an archeological grant to Egypt. Nick visits London Dictionary and finds out about Henson Manufacturing.

In the evening, Rosie gets followed into an alleway where she witnesses something terrifying. She narrowly escapes some thugs that corner her by jumping through a window. In the meantime, Abdul tracks a car into Limehouse Docks.

Wednesday, 4. February 1925

England - London

Abdul catches a cold from staying in the rain overnight. The investigators revisit London Dictionary to acquire about Limehouse Docks and Ferrys & Sons. The trio stocks up on binoculars and goes to stake out Penhew Foundation.

Thursday, 5. February 1925

England - Limehouse Docks

Nick gets into trouble in Limehouse Docks and gets locked up. Abdul rushes in to save Nick, Rosie and Joseph following in his tail. The team barely escapes by blowing up the warehouse and causing confusion. They steal some dynamite sticks for later use.

In the evening, the investigators plan a break in to Penhew Foundation. They plant a diary and offer some laces smokes to the guard.

Friday, 6. February 1925

England - Penhew Foundation

In the evening, the investigators plan a break-in to Penhew Foundation. They plant a diary and offer some laces smokes to the guard. At midnight, the trio breaks in and discovers a secret room in the basement.

Friday, 6. February 1925

England - Leichester

The investigators travel to Leichester.

Saturday, 7. February 1925

England - Leichester

Rosie gets an intricate golden mask from Nick and experiences a meeting with the Crawling Chaos.

Sunday, 8. February 1925

England - Derby

The group travels to Derby. Joseph buys a car. The settle in to a barn next to Henson Manufacturing to learn more. Abdul and Rosie try to get workers drunk at a pub. Nick and Joseph stake out Henson Manufacturing.

Monday, 9. February 1925

England - Derby

Edward Gavigan visits Henson Manufacturing. As it seems that a delivery is soon to be made, Nick comes up with a plan to intercept the truck. Joseph sabotages the cars of Mr. Gavigan and his associates. The investigators form an ambush on the road to London. The plan goes extremely well and the delivery is intercepted, the truck blown up with Mr. Gavigan trapped inside.

Tuesday, 10. February 1925

England - Derby

In the evening, Joseph and Nick break in to Henson Manufacturing and manage to apprehend the guards. They break into the walk-in safe and manage to steal some impressive blueprints.

Tuesday, 10. February 1925

England - Colchester

The investigators drive to Colchester. Abdul researches the nearby maps and nature.

Wednesday, 11. February 1925

England - Colchester

A package and a letter is sent to Ramsey. Joseph travels to London under cover and buys a camera. Joseph visits Scoop and finds out that Rosie and Nick are considered fugitives.

Thursday, 12. February 1925

England - Walton-on-the-Naze

The group travels to Walton-on-the-Naze and acquires two motor boats. Nick and Joseph meet local farmers. Rosie and Abdul travel to the Misr House.

Friday, 13. February 1925

England - Walton-on-the-Naze

The group decides to stake out Misr House. Nick and Joseph witness a brutal ritual by Zahra Shafik. Rosie gets attacked by the ritual and falls unconscious. Joseph, Nick and Abdul fight their way through the cultists. They discover an initiate called Mary and manage to change her ways. In the basement, the group finds Liam who has been tortured for a week. Liam takes everyone back to London into the safety of a Nexus.
